computer system component explaination

As an officer working in the IT department at a company, understanding the components that have been used in a system is important, UpToDate knowledge of system’s component is also as important as understanding the uses of the components.

In today’s technology world most company uses custom made systems this is to synchronize company’s system, having a custom build PC makes the company saves of maintenance budget as these custom build PCs is easily maintained and easily upgradeable.

in order to build a working and useable system a technician need to connect and assemble components that consist of: -

Processor 

Motherboard

Power Supply

Hard Disk & Disk Configuration 

Internal Memory Configuration 

PCIe Cards

Ports 

 Heat Sink and Fan (cooling solutions)
