Ports

Article header with background image and parallax effect

a. As for ports the motherboard always comes with its ready built boards, these ports are the ones responsible for connection between BIOS.

b. Ports are used in many different situations in a motherboard the provided ports are; HDMI, DVI, 4 USB3.0, Thunderbolt, USB-C, audio input and output, Ethernet and PS/2 Mini DIN -6. (ports provided may vary between brands).

c. Extra ports also comes with the casing for the PC this may also vary on casing brand.

Heat Sink and Fan
(cooling solutions)

Article header with background image and parallax effect

a. Heat sink is a very important aspect of a computer system as it keeps the system cooled this will affect the performance of a system.

b. Cooling solutions are in wide range as it remains for the client to choose, some may prefer water cooled some may choose fan cooled and some just stick with the default solution.

c. With a default solution the user can reduce the build cost as the cooling system for the processor are provided when bought.
