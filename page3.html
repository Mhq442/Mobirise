Supply Unit (PSU)

a. Power supply unit is an electrical device that supplies electrical power to a system, without a Power Supply the machine cannot be turned on. It converts mains AC (alternating current) to a low – voltage regulated DC (direct current) power for the internal components of a computer.

b. Voltage may vary according to its usage for the system, more complicated hardware is used the more power usage will the system need.
